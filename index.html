    About Alzheimer's and Dementia

    Alzheimer's disease is a form of dementia. There is no cure, but there are treatments that can help manage symptons. Symptoms include: difficulties problem solving, confusion with time or place, personality and mood changes, trouble speaking, walking, or swallowing.
